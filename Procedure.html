One-Time Pad Cipher

PROCEDURE:

For ENCRYPTION:
STEP 1 : Select a Equal Lenght plain text and a key by clicking on the "Run Encrypt test" buttons .
STEP 2:Convert into codebook(0-99) equalavent code of plaintext.
STEP 2: Convert Text plain-text & key to equivalencent binary 8-bit value.
STEP 3 :XORing 8-bit plain-text and 8-bit key.
STEP 4:We get 8-bit Encrypted(or Cipher-bits).
STEP 5:Convert 8-bit cipher text to text format of cipher text.
STEP 6:Convert into codebook(0-99) equalavent code of cipher-text.
STEP 7:We get get expected cipher text(Encrypted text).

FOR DECRYPTION:
STEP 1:Select a Equal Lenght Cipher text and a key by clicking on the "Run Decrypt test" buttons
STEP 6:Convert into codebook(0-99) equalavent code of cipher-text.
STEP 2:Convert Text cipher-text & key to equivalencent binary 8-bit value.
STEP 3:XORing 8-bit Cipher-text and 8-bit key.
STEP 4:We get 8-bit Decrypted(or plaintext-bits).
STEP 5:Convert 8-bit plaintext text to text format of cipher text.
STEP 6:Convert into codebook(0-99) equalavent code of plaintext.
STEP 6:We get get expected plain-text(Decrypted text).
